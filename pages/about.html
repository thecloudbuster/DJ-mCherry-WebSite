DJ mCherry // Agustín Alonso

DJ, Producer, Visual Artist

mCherry (aka Cherry KFC) is an Argentinian born, Montréal-based DJ, and producer.

His sound dwells in the intersection between cumbia and techno, and recurring incursions into samba, baile funk, vogue femme beats and latin club.

His sets mix high doses of euphoria and nostalgia, bringing a dample of argentinian nightlife to the northern canadian latitudes.

During his time in montreal he has also established himself as a ballroom DJ, incorporating cunty performance beats to his palette of sounds.


He is the co-founder of the haus of KFC, a kiki house involved in ballroom and immigrant-oriented non-profit organization.

He also produces the monthly event Pikete, an itinerant party destined to immigrant latinx talent and sound representation.
